Motivations

As neurodegenerative disorders rise, there is a clear need for early-onset therapy and prevention. With the recent improvements in diagnostic technologies, a new standard of care is available. BrainTrain dawns at the new age of digital therapeutics. In this new age, treatments rely on the electromagnetic nature of the human body. This allows for patient-specific treatment and optimization with minimal side effects compared to current standards of care.

EEG

Electroencephalogram, better known as EEG is a non-invasive technology used to record and track brain wave patterns. These patterns emerge as a result of the underlying neuronal oscillations. BrainTrain device reads and analyzes the underlying neuronal activity produced by the brain. Using this information to guide and optimize non-invasive technologies.

  • Learn more

tACS

Transcranial cranial alternating current stimulation involves the application of low level electrical current to the scalp. Such a low current is barely perceptible. With the application of specifically generated waveforms, BrainTrain has the functional capability to interact with underlying neuronal activity. When integrated alongside EEG, a level of capability is achieved unlike anything else in the market.

What we do

Through a synthesis of robust engineering disciplines, we are developing a revolutionary piece of biotechnology. A combination of materials engineering, digital signal processing, hardware design, and computational modeling are utilized by our team of biomedical engineers.

Materials Engineering

Current materials used by devices in the space are not optimized for user experience. Our team of engineers is currently developing novel electrode designs to decrease set-up times, reduce signal attenuation, and enhance user experience.

Digital Signal Processing

Valuable and actionable insights are provided via the electrical signals recorded of underlying neuronal oscillations. While these signals are obtained by the EEG, complex DSP techniques are being developed.

Hardware Design

A specialized approach to hardware design separates our project from the market and allows for real-time EEG+tACS integration. Currently, there are no other products on the market with the descibed functionality.

Computational Modeling

We are solving complex problems and generating models with accuracy and speed with the help of finite element simulations, and machine learning algorithms. A combination of biomedical imaging modalities is utilized to perfect the design of the BrainTrain device.
